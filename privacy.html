Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 07, 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how Kemetica collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our language-learning app focused on hieroglyphics and ancient Egyptian culture. By using our service, you agree to the terms described here.

Interpretation and Definitions

Definitions

For the purpose of this Privacy Policy:

  • Account means a unique account created for you to access our Service.
  • Application refers to Kemetica, our language-learning app.
  • Company (referred to as "We", "Us", or "Our") refers to Kemetica.
  • Personal Data means any information related to an identifiable individual, such as email, name, and usage data.
  • Service refers to the Kemetica app.
  • Usage Data refers to data automatically collected when using the Service.
  • You refers to the individual or entity accessing our Service.

Collecting and Using Your Data

Personal Data

We collect personal data to provide and improve the Kemetica app. This data includes:

  • Email address
  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Birthdate
  • Gender
  • Experience points for progress tracking

Usage Data

We collect only the personal information necessary to enhance your experience with Kemetica. This information helps us personalize your learning journey and track your progress effectively.

Use of Your Data

Your personal data is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the app
  • Manage user accounts and facilitate sign-ins
  • Contact you with relevant updates
  • Analyze and improve our services

Data Retention

Your personal data is retained as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Usage data is typically retained for a shorter duration, except when required for security purposes.

Children's Privacy

Kemetica is a language-learning app with a focus on ancient Egyptian culture. While the app is educational, it is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Security of Data

We take the security of your personal data seriously but note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We use reasonable means to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly.
